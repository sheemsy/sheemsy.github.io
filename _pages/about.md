I am an integrated Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering at Korea University, working on multimodal intelligence and human-centered AI systems.

My recent work focuses on wearable sensing, driver state understanding, and multimodal learning for real-world interactive systems.

Featured Project
======
[MUST: Smartwatch-based Multimodal Framework for Predicting Driver State and Takeover Performance](https://mintlab.korea.ac.kr/MUST/) was accepted to CHI 2026. The project introduces a smartwatch-based multimodal framework for predicting driver state and takeover performance in conditionally autonomous driving scenarios.

You can find the full project page here:
- [https://mintlab.korea.ac.kr/MUST/](https://mintlab.korea.ac.kr/MUST/)
