      <h2 class="research-home__section-title">MUST</h2>
      <p>MUST is a smartwatch-based multimodal framework for predicting driver state and takeover performance in conditionally autonomous driving. The work shows how unobtrusive wearable sensing and multimodal fusion can support readiness assessment without intrusive hardware or fragile vision-only setups.</p>
